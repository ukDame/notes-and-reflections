# Notes & Reflections - Cancer Journey Support Tool

A gentle, supportive journaling tool designed for cancer patients and their loved ones to pause, reflect, and gather their thoughts during challenging times.

## üìã What's Included

This repository contains two versions of the Notes & Reflections tool:

1. **PDF Version** (`Notes_and_Reflections.pdf`)
   - Downloadable and printable
   - Can be filled in digitally or printed for handwriting
   - Perfect for those who prefer physical journaling or don't always have internet access

2. **Interactive Web Version** (`Notes_and_Reflections_Interactive.html`)
   - Complete online experience
   - Auto-saves your reflections as you type (stored locally in your browser)
   - Download your reflections as a text file
   - Print-friendly
   - Mobile responsive
   - No internet required after initial download

## üí≠ Reflection Prompts

The tool includes 10 thoughtfully designed prompts:

1. What I want to remember
2. What am I learning about myself through this experience?
3. What I'm grateful for today
4. Who or what has been my biggest source of support?
5. Questions I want to ask my doctor or nurse
6. What small victory or achievement can I celebrate today?
7. What helped me relax recently
8. What brings me comfort or peace?
9. What do I need to let go of or release right now?
10. A message to my future self

## üöÄ How to Use

### PDF Version
1. Download `Notes_and_Reflections.pdf`
2. Open in any PDF reader
3. Fill in digitally or print and write by hand

### Interactive Web Version
1. Download `Notes_and_Reflections_Interactive.html`
2. Open in any web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
3. Start writing - your reflections auto-save
4. Download as text file or print anytime
5. **Privacy Note:** All data is stored locally in your browser - nothing is sent to any server

## üåê Making the Interactive Version Accessible Online
